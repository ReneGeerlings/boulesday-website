Frequently asked questions

Answers to what tournament leaders often want to know before trying BoulesDay.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. You need nothing during the tournament. iCloud syncs in the background when you do have a connection — otherwise just later.

Do the players need the app too?

No. Only you as the tournament leader. Players see their round, lane and opponent on the scorecard or on your iPad.

What if I have an odd number of teams?

The app shares out byes fairly. Each round, one team sits out. That counts as a won match with the score 13–6, following NJBB rules.

Can I correct a score if I make a mistake?

Yes. Clear a score and re-enter it. The standings update straight away.

Does printing work on a regular printer?

Yes. Schedule, scorecards, standings and final results print as A4 — via AirPrint, or as PDF to share.

How do I switch the app to Dutch?

Through iOS settings: open Settings → BoulesDay → Language and pick Dutch. The app picks up the change right away. Switch back to English in the same place.
